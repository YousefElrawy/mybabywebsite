HealthCare Services

How Can I Keep My Child Healthy?

Ensure proper nutrition, personal hygiene, timely vaccinations, and regular check-ups with the doctor to monitor your child’s growth and health.

When should I book an appointment with a pediatrician ?

You should book an appointment if you notice unusual symptoms such as a persistent high fever, strange rashes, or changes in your child’s activity. Additionally, regular check-ups are recommended to ensure your child’s health.

How can I deal with a fever in children ?

You can use cold compresses, ensure your child stays hydrated, and dress them in lightweight clothing. If the fever persists for more than two days or rises above 39°C, consult a doctor immediately.

What are the essential vaccinations for my child ?

The essential vaccinations include those against measles, polio, diphtheria, whooping cough, and hepatitis. Refer to the recommended vaccination schedule from the Ministry of Health or your pediatrician.
